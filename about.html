About Keep Bank — Training Lab

Keep Bank is a fictional institution used solely for educational exercises in networking and security labs. Below is example accounting/banking information you can reference during your practicals.

Sample Products & Accounting Concepts

  • Accounts: Checking, Savings, Fixed Deposits — basic ledger behavior and flows.
  • Interest: Periodic crediting, simple vs compound interest (illustrative rates shown).
  • Loans: Amortization schedules, principal vs interest, monthly payment calculations.
  • Statements: Transaction entries, running balance, debit/credit conventions.

Why this helps your lab

Using this mock site you can safely practice capturing HTTP POST requests, understanding where credentials and form data appear in network packets, and comparing that to secure channels (HTTPS) in a controlled setting.
