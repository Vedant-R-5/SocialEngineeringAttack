Fictional products for UI practice

The options below are not real financial products. They exist to help you build and demo a professional-looking banking interface.

Everyday Account (Demo)

A typical current/checking account layout with balance, transactions, and basic controls.

  • Sample IBAN / account number formatting
  • Fake transaction list for UI demos
  • Balance and limit indicators

Use this to showcase home dashboards and summary widgets.

Savings Goals (Demo)

Goal-based savings visuals, using progress bars and timelines instead of real interest calculations.

  • Progress bars and goal percentages
  • Goal cards with statuses
  • Fake contribution schedule

Ideal for testing charts, interactions, and motivational copy.

Virtual Card (Demo)

A UI-only representation of a card that never touches real payment networks.

  • Card masking patterns
  • Design experiments (gradients, glass, etc.)
  • Lock/unlock toggle simulations

Great for accessibility checks and hover/tap states.

Loans Overview (Demo)

A simple mock loans page where you can show repayment schedules, but all numbers are fictional.

  • Monthly repayment tiles
  • Remaining balance progress
  • Sample interest rate labels

Use to demonstrate complex financial layouts without real underwriting.

Multi-currency Wallet (Demo)

Fake balances in multiple currencies to test currency switchers and responsive tables.

  • USD / EUR / GBP sample balances
  • Static “rate” indicators
  • Flag icons and currency labels

Useful for localization and formatting practice.

Notifications Center (Demo)

A notification feed UI that mimics typical banking alerts in a safe, fake environment.

  • Security alerts samples
  • Payment reminders
  • Marketing messages

Practice filtering, grouping, and read/unread states.

Hook your own logic if needed

You can connect these demo products to your own front-end logic in script.js to animate numbers, filter lists, or simulate flows. Just remember:

  • Do not connect to real customer data.
  • Do not collect real banking credentials.
  • Keep “Demo / Sample / Fake” labeling visible.
